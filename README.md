# Big Billion Day Sale - Data Analysis

A comprehensive analysis of 11,239 customer transactions from a major e-commerce Big Billion Days Sale campaign. This project examines customer demographics, geographic distribution, product performance, and purchasing behavior to provide actionable business intelligence.

## Dataset
- **Total Transactions:** 11,239
- **Variables:** 13 (Customer info, Geography, Products, Transactions)
- **Analysis Type:** Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) with visualizations

## Key Findings

**Customers:** Female buyers (26-35 years) dominate with significantly higher revenue. Married customers show stronger purchase values.

**Geography:** Uttar Pradesh leads in sales, followed by Maharashtra and Karnataka. Central and Western zones are primary markets.

**Products:** Food category generates highest revenue, followed by Clothing & Apparel and Electronics.

**Purchases:** Average transaction ₹9,453.61 with customers placing 2-3 orders on average. Transaction range ₹188-₹23,952.

## Tech Stack
Python, Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, Seaborn

## Key Insights
- Primary target: Female customers aged 26-35
- Top states: Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka  
- Revenue driver: Food category
- Opportunity: Increase order frequency through loyalty programs

## Recommendations
- Focus marketing on female segment (26-35 age group)
- Strengthen inventory in Food category
- Target married customers with premium offers
- Expand in secondary states
- Implement occupation-based campaigns
